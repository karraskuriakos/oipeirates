Extension Name: Web-App Mode
Extension URI: http://www.wptouch.com/extensions/web-app-mode
Author: BraveNewCode Inc.
Description: Add the ability for Android and iOS visitors to save your website like an app to the device homescreen, loading without the browser.
Version: 1.1
Depends-on: 4.0

== Long Description ==

Offer an app without the App Store. Only WPtouch allows your iOS visitors to save your website like an app. Web-App Mode is a unique feature that enables your WPtouch-powered website to be saved to iOS and Android visitors' home screens, like an app. Once they open your app, they'll see your mobile website without the browser chrome. They can browse through your website in an immersive, browser-free way, enhancing their mobile experience and enjoyment of your content.
